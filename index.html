My Top 3 Music Genres

Jazz Indie Rock Hide/Show Fav Artists

Jazz

Jazz music is possibly the most captivating musical genre of all time. I started playing jazz in middle school and honestly wasn't really a fan as my exposure to it was hearing our school band and other school bands play at a mediocre level. Once I had the experience of witnessing a professional jazz band play, I finally was hooked. The raw talent that jazz musicians exude on stage is incredible.

My Favorite Jazz Artists

Victor Wooten
Buddy Rich
Jaco Pastorius

Indie

Indie is a genre I didn't fully start immersing myself in until well after I was an adult. I started teaching myself the guitar, and after starting to learn songs at home I fell in love with Indie and Acoustic music. There are so many Indie artists to see locally here in Utah, and the community is honestly one of the best parts. I've been to dozens of Indie music festivals, open mics, large conerts, and even a few 'sleepover concerts'.

My Favorite Indie Artists

Sufjan Stevens
City and Colour
Noah Kahan

Rock

Rock is the biggest constant in my life. I was raised on classic rock by my dad and attended countless rock concerts before I was evern a teenager. My love of rock has expanded greatly to also include every variety including garage, punk, alt, and even hard rock/metal. When I'm sad I listen to rock. When I'm working out I listen to rock. While my other music tastes have varied and changed, this has been my main staple in my music my whole life.

My Favorite Rock Artists

Queens of the Stone Age
Cleopatrick
The White Stripes
